We are on the final stretch toward the 26.04 release on **April 23rd**, and this week brought a satisfying round of long-standing annoyances getting squashed. A huge thank you to everyone who tested, patched, and shipped.

## Thank you to our contributors

**Thank you to Erich of Ubuntu Studio** for addressing our spammy notifications issue and for his do-release-upgrade work — both very much appreciated.

**Thank you to KFocus** for the shiny new theme update and accompanying fixes.

**Thank you to Rik** — for everything, and for uploading the Plasma 6.6.4 bug fix release.

## Come help us test

As we close in on release, testers are very welcome. Come join the conversation:

- **Development:** [#kubuntu-devel:ubuntu.com](https://matrix.to/#/#kubuntu-devel:ubuntu.com) on Matrix
- **Support:** [#kubuntu-support:ubuntu.com](https://matrix.to/#/#kubuntu-support:ubuntu.com) on Matrix

In my not-so-spare time, I am also working on 26.04 beta snaps for KDE Applications — testers welcome there too.
